WIND TELECOM, S. A. is a telecommunications company in the Dominican Republic focused on delivering integrated Voice, Digital Television, and Internet services to residential customers, as well as specialized services to businesses, institutions, and other service providers, guaranteeing quality, cost, and delivery.

Its voice and data network is fully IP-based and relies primarily on WiMax technology, supported by point-to-point microwave links and fiber-optic networks for transporting large volumes of information. Its video network is based on MMDS (Multichannel Multipoint Distribution System).

Its purpose is to be the preferred provider of connectivity, information content, and entertainment solutions across the residential, business, and institutional markets, meeting customer expectations for quality, cost, and delivery while directly supporting education in the Dominican Republic.
